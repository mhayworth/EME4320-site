Welcome to Geneartive AI Fundamentals!

Learn to Enhance Your Classroom with AI

Many people feel uncertain about the future of AI. But in reality, it has many important applications that can improve all of our lives. In this course, we will be exploring how generative AI can enhance the classroom environment. We will look at how generative AI systems are developed and prompted through Natural Langauge Processing (NLP). Then, we will explore using generative AI tools to create new assignments and convert single-media presentations into more engaging multimedia instructional materials. Finally, we will examine the importance of ethics in developing, training, and applying generative AI in both educational and enterprise environments. After this course, you should be able to apply these fundamentals to your own classroom to create an enhanced multimedia learning environment powered by AI.
