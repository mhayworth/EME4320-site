Learn to Use Multimodal Machine Learning Methods

Many people feel uncertain about the future of AI. But in reality, it has many important applications that can improve all of our lives. In this course, we will be exploring a subfield of AI called multimodal machine learning (ML). We will use multiple methods, ranging from simple libraries to complex machine learning frameworks such as TensorFlow. Additionally, we will explore how to properly prepare and label data for supervised machine learning. The course will have multimedia content including images, videos, and audio tutorials about multimodal ML. After this course, you should be able to apply these fundamentals to your own datasets and build a system that can make predictions - your own AI! Finally, we will overview current AI research and applications, from image recognition in medical applications to helpful assistants like ChatGPT.
