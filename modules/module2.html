Module End Activities

AI Integration Assignment: Write a brief (<1 page) reflection on your own classroom environment. Are there any areas where students may be using AI to circumvent the course design? How can you flip the script to make AI a companion in this area and prepare the students for an AI-augmented future? Try to think beyond simple assignments where students critique the work of AI. Instead, consider where the student can use AI as a thinking, doing, or creating companion.

Content Enhancing Assessment: Find a learning resource that is currently only one mode of learning such as a chapter from a textbook you currently use. Use at least two types of generative AI (textual, image, or audio) to create a multimedia presentation where AI helps depict topics, provide clarification, or speaks to highlight key points. The end result should be a presentation that takes about 15 minutes. Then, prompt ChatGPT or another LLM with a summary of your presentation and ask for a follow-up activity for students. Write a brief (<1 page) analysis of this proposed assignment and determine whether it fits your classroom needs.
